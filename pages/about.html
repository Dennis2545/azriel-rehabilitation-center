Background

Alzeria is established as a focal point of trust, recuperation and strengthening for individuals with liquor and substance misuse reliance, and individuals with psychological instabilities. Its key goal hence is to give a tranquil climate to treatment and recuperation by giving aftercare and follow-up programs for strengthening to the victims and the impacted populace of families, the general public and country at large. The Tranquility Spot will try to work with the contaminated and impacted people, with networks and partners since compulsion is an illness so it becomes preventable, treatable and sensible at all levels. We will offer individualized help for individuals with a wide range of psychological instabilities. We are Enlisted by the important legal bodies and team up with similar accomplices to accomplish our goals
